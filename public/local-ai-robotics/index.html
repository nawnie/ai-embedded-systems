Local AI Robotics

Local AI robotics work at AI Embedded Systems centers on autonomy that runs onboard. We care about systems that can perceive, remember, reason, and act in real time when a network connection is unavailable or inappropriate.

  • Local autonomy bounded by explicit safety rules
  • On-device model runtime measured on target hardware
  • Robot memory and telemetry with retention limits
  • Decision support that preserves operator authority

Local does not mean isolated

A robot may need to keep working through poor connectivity, but it still needs controlled updates, useful logs, and a way for people to understand its state. Local AI is most valuable when it reduces a real operational dependency without hiding risk inside the machine.

We scope the smallest offline-capable behavior worth proving, then measure model runtime, sensor-to-action delay, recovery behavior, and what information must leave the device. That evidence supports a clear decision about whether to continue, change hardware, or simplify the goal.
