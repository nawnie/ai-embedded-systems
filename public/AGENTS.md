## Company Summary

AI Embedded Systems designs private AI, embedded systems, robotics, and practical automation for real-world operations. The company helps operations-heavy organizations identify useful AI opportunities, understand technical and security constraints, and move toward implementation without sending every decision or dataset to a remote cloud service.

RNV1 robotics development provides hands-on physical-world engineering experience in sensing, local compute, perception, control, and embedded autonomy.
