A first decision before a large build

Robotics projects become expensive when hardware, software, AI, and operational assumptions move forward without a shared test. We start by naming the workflow, users, environment, constraints, and decision owner. That makes it possible to choose evidence that will actually change a business or engineering decision.

The Private AI Feasibility Sprint can produce a technical fit assessment, data-flow view, risk register, and bounded prototype plan. When a live demonstration is appropriate, we define the smallest useful behavior and the evidence needed to continue or stop.
