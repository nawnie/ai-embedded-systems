RNV1 Robotics

RNV1 is our hands-on robotics engineering platform for physical-world intelligence. It gives us a concrete place to test how sensing, onboard compute, local AI, control, telemetry, and operator oversight fit together before recommending those patterns to a client.

  • Prototype architecture with explicit module boundaries
  • Sensor validation against the operating environment
  • Onboard compute, control, and health reporting
  • Field test planning with observable pass criteria

What the platform is for

RNV1 is not presented as a finished commercial product. It is an engineering platform used to uncover integration problems early: timing, power, connectivity, sensor disagreement, recovery behavior, and the gap between a lab demonstration and repeatable operation.

A useful engagement starts with the environment and the decision the machine must support. From there we can identify the smallest hardware-and-software slice worth testing, define how success will be measured, and make the risks visible before a larger build.
